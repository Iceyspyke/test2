Legal Standards Reference

Genocide Convention, Art. II

Acts committed with intent to destroy, in whole or in part, a national, ethnical, racial or religious group.

Rome Statute, Art. 7

Crimes Against Humanity: widespread or systematic attack directed against any civilian population.

Apartheid Convention, Art. II

Inhuman acts committed for the purpose of establishing and maintaining domination by one racial group.
