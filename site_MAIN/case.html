The Indictment

The Application alleges that acts and omissions by the Respondent are committed with the requisite specific intent to destroy, in whole or in part, Palestinians in Gaza as a part of the broader Palestinian national, racial and ethnical group.

Count I

Genocide

Systematic destruction of a national group through military operations, starvation, and displacement.

Count II

War Crimes

Direct attacks on civilians and civilian objects, disproportionate force, and use of prohibited weapons.

Count III

Apartheid

Institutionalized regime of systematic oppression and domination by one racial group over another.

Count IV

Crimes Against Humanity

Extermination, enslavement, deportation or forcible transfer of population.

Count V

Manufactured Starvation

The deliberate use of starvation of civilians as a method of warfare by depriving them of objects indispensable to their survival, including willfully impeding relief supplies as provided for under the Geneva Conventions.

Final Verdicts & Orders

Order 01

Immediate Cessation of Military Offensive

The Court indicates that the Respondent shall, in accordance with its obligations under the Convention on the Prevention and Punishment of the Crime of Genocide, and in view of the worsening conditions of life faced by civilians, immediately halt its military offensive.

Order 02

Unhindered Provision of Humanitarian Aid

Ensure the unhindered provision at scale by all concerned of urgently needed basic services and humanitarian assistance, including food, water, electricity, fuel, shelter, clothing, hygiene and sanitation requirements.

Order 03

Preservation of Evidence

The Respondent shall take effective measures to prevent the destruction and to ensure the preservation of evidence related to allegations of acts within the scope of Article II and Article III of the Genocide Convention.

Order 04

Mandatory Compliance Reporting

The Respondent must submit a comprehensive report to the Court on all measures taken to give effect to this Order within one month as from the date of this Order.
