Archival census data documenting the systematic erasure of over 530 Palestinian towns and villages. Data cross-referenced with British Mandate records and UN Conciliation Commission files.

DEPOPULATION METRICSSOURCE: UN CONCILIATION COMMISSION
Forensic mapping of the 1948 Nakba reveals the intentional demolition of civilian infrastructure to prevent the return of refugees. Urban centers were ethnically cleansed, and rural agricultural villages were bulldozed and subsequently afforested (planted over with non-native pine trees) to conceal architectural ruins.
