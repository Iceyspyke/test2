The Chronology of Occupation & Resistance

An unyielding ledger of the historical process. This document maps the displacement and the enduring resilience of the Palestinian people from the early 20th century to the present.

METHODOLOGY TIMELINE PARAMETERS
The following chronological ledger cross-references British imperial documents, UN mandates, declassified military directives, and verified oral histories. It establishes the legal and physical mechanisms used to alter the demographic and territorial reality of Palestine over the course of a century.
