The separation wall cut directly through our olive groves. Now, we are required to apply for agricultural permits to access our own land. Last harvest, my permit was denied for 'unspecified security reasons.' The olives rotted on the branches while the settlement across the valley expanded.
Mahmoud F. — Landowner
Transcript filed under Exhibit C: Economic Asphyxiation and De Facto Annexation. Corroborated by satellite telemetry showing 400 dunams of isolated agricultural land.
