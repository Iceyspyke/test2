Privacy Protocol

To protect the identities of whistleblowers, embedded journalists, and civilian sources, all direct identifiers in unverified logs have been scrubbed. Node connections are anonymized, and no tracking cookies or telemetry data are stored on user hardware.

Universal Jurisdiction Protocols

In accordance with the Geneva Conventions, grave breaches of international humanitarian law trigger universal jurisdiction. This mechanism obligates all signatory states to search for, arrest, and prosecute individuals suspected of orchestrating or carrying out war crimes, regardless of their nationality or where the crimes were committed. These archives serve as an evidentiary backbone for prosecutors invoking these articles internationally.
